A Sinusoidal function

Transformation: I chose this photo because I often eat ramyeon as lunch near the Sunway’s convenience store. To model the sign appearing in this photo, I started to walk around the campus and observe the small things. Then I realized that the ramyeon noodle looks similar to the sinusoidal faction so I selected the parent function y=sinx as a starting point.
